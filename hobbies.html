Hobbies and Interests

Biking

Most of the biking I do is of the mountain variety. Oddly enough, there are no mountains around here, so maybe it's more trail biking. Either way, there are some amazing trail systems all around Houston. My favorite is the Cypresswood Trail in Collins Park all the way up in Spring. For info about all the trails around Houston, you can visit the Greater Houston Off Road Biking Association's website.


Drum Corps

When I was in high school, I played in the drumline in the high school band. From being involved in band, I was introduced to a summer long, extra-curricular band activity called Drum Corps. Basically, it's kind of like an advanced marching band comprised of mostly college age performers. Nerd alert! I was a member of the Blue Knights from Denver and the Santa Clara Vanguard from Santa Clara, CA. Below is video from 2004 with Santa Clara Vanguard.



When you turn 21, you "age out" of drum corps. After I aged out, I went on to teach the Bluecoats from Canton, OH and the Cavaliers from Rosemont, IL.
